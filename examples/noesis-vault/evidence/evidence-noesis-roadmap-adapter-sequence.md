# Noesis Roadmap Adapter Sequence Evidence

## Evidence

The checked-in docs describe the vault as the durable source of truth and
describe CLI, MCP, and portable skills as adapters over the same parser,
validator, lineage tracer, review queue, context builder, and lifecycle write
functions.

## Source Basis

- [[source-noesis-roadmap-docs]]

## Extraction Notes

This evidence focuses on sequencing roadmap work. It does not claim that every
future adapter already exists or that a custom Obsidian plugin is permanently
unnecessary.

## Candidate Claims

- Next-phase planning should mature the file-backed project-memory corpus and
  adapter workflows before moving schema authority into a custom app surface.
